El Pollo Loco

Rules

Collect as many salsa bottles as possible.

Use the bottles to defeat Señora Gallina, the crazy giant chicken. Throw at least 5 bottles at her.

Fight her crazy chickens by jumping on them.

Collect the little chicks to gain new health points. So be careful, not to jump on.

Collect coins to earn some extra points.
